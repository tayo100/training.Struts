User interface concepts

ProMIS CRM is designed for simple navigation through data managed by the system. Clicking on links will bring you to a profile page where more details are available.

Dashboard

Dashboard is your entry point to ProMIS CRM. Depending on your level of access, you may see:

  • C&GO Dashboard
  • Finance office dashboard
  • User's dashboard

Each of these are tailored to provide data most relevant to your role.
